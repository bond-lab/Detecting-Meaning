The third project for HG8011: Detecting Meaning with Sherlock Holmes.

The format is

  • The id is for internal use, please remember it and your password
  • Clicking on the sentences jumps you to the start sentence (with 4 sentences before and after)
    These are different sentences from projects 1 and 2
  • You will have to log in each time you change to a new browser
  • Don't tag past the end sentence (there will not be a warning)
  • Sentences vary in length, so some have more, some less, but you all have around 450 concepts.
  • If you have not yet done so, read the whole story: The Hound of the Baskervilles (HOUN)
  • Tag sentiment for all words that need to be tagged in the sentences assigned to you.
    • Most words (85–90%), will be neutral, but some words (10–15%) convey positive or negative sentiment
    • More detailed instructions are given in the » Simple Sentiment Guide «
    • The tool works best under Chrome or Firefox.
    • You will be assessed on
      • Completion: is everything that shows sentiment tagged?
      • Recall: have you only tagged things that show sentiment?
      • Accuracy: are the tags reasonable? (do they agree more than a threshold with an expert annotation)
  • Estimated time 2-5 hours (but it may take longer). Don't overthink things.
  • You should have checked every word by the due date.
  • Comment words where you are not sure of the sentiment
  • You can change sense tags, but are not encouraged to.
